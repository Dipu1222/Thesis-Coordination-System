Key Features

Everything you need for a successful thesis journey in one platform

👥

Team Formation

Easily find and form teams with your classmates. Send requests, manage team members, and collaborate effectively.

🎓

Supervisor Assignment

Browse available supervisors, check their research areas, and send requests. Get assigned to the best match for your project.

Document Management

Upload proposals, reports, and presentations. Track submission status and receive feedback directly on the platform.
