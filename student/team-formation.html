Team Formation Guidelines

Team Rules

  • • Maximum team size: 3 students
  • • All members must be from same department
  • • Team leader responsible for submissions
  • • Formation must complete before supervisor selection

Formation Process

  1. Find classmates using search
  2. Send team requests to members
  3. Wait for acceptance
  4. Select supervisor after formation

Important Notes

  • • Only one team per student
  • • Team leader cannot be changed
  • • Team disbanded if leader leaves
  • • Finalize within first 2 weeks
