Selection Guidelines
  • Each supervisor can supervise maximum 10 teams
  • Check supervisor's research areas before sending request
  • You will be notified when supervisor accepts/rejects your request
  • Once accepted, you can submit annex form
  • Supervisor allocation is based on first-come-first-served basis
  • You can withdraw your request before acceptance
  • Ensure your team is fully formed before requesting supervisors
